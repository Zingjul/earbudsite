# Apple Watch Ultra 2 - Brand & Design Guidelines

This document provides a breakdown of the brand design, aesthetic choices, and color palette based on the visual style of the Apple Watch Ultra 2 product imagery. This information is structured to act as a definitive guide for LLMs or designers to replicate the tone, look, and feel of this specific interface.

## 1. Visual Aesthetic & Tone

*   **Vibe:** Premium, modern, elite, highly technical yet accessible.
*   **Aesthetic:** "Dark Mode" native, minimalist, high contrast, product-centric.
*   **Layout Strategy:** Generous negative space (pitch black backgrounds) to guide focus entirely onto the bold product imagery and key data points. Center-aligned hero elements with clean, subtle top navigation.

## 2. Typography Strategy

*   **Font Family:** Clean, geometric, modern sans-serif (similar to Apple's *San Francisco / SF Pro*, particularly using *SF Compact* for watch metrics).
*   **Hierarchy:**
    *   *Hero/Display:* Bold, massive, high-contrast numerals (e.g., the watch face time).
    *   *Headers:* Stark white, medium-to-bold weight for brand names.
    *   *Navigation/Muted details:* Lighter, thinner weights in gray to recede into the background.
    *   *Technical Data:* Condensed metrics, sharp and legible.

## 3. UI Component Guidelines

*   **Buttons:** Pill-shaped, fully rounded corners (e.g., the "Pre-order" button). Filled with a highly visible accent color (blue), no borders, white text.
*   **Navigation:** Minimalist top bar, horizontally aligned, with equal spacing. Uses muted text colors until hovered/active to avoid distracting from the hero product.
*   **Data Visualization (Complications):** Layered, circular dials and simple typography. Brightly colored data points juxtaposed against dark gray or black backgrounds within the UI components.

## 4. Exact Color Palette

*(Note: Hex Codes are high-fidelity estimates based on the visual reference)*

### Base & Typography Colors
*   **Primary Background:** `#000000` (Pitch Black)
    *   *Usage:* Global background. Creates infinite depth and allows colorful elements to pop.
*   **Primary Text & Highlights:** `#FFFFFF` (Pure White)
    *   *Usage:* Main titles, dominant watch face numbers, user-focused data, and brand logos.
*   **Secondary Text & UI Elements:** `#86868B` (Muted Slate Gray)
    *   *Usage:* Top navigation links, inactive states, and secondary sub-headers.

### Accent & Action Colors
*   **Action Blue:** `#0071E3` (Vibrant Brand Blue)
    *   *Usage:* Reserved specifically for primary call-to-action elements, like the solid "Pre-order" button.
*   **Technical Orange:** `#FF453A` (High-Vis Alert Orange)
    *   *Usage:* Specialized branding (e.g., the "ULTRA 2" text) and critical metrics or active indicators on the technical watch face.
*   **Metric Cyan/Light Blue:** `#32ADE6` (Luminous Cyan)
    *   *Usage:* Used extensively within data-heavy UI components (like the watch face) to highlight environmental data such as altitude, depth, and gauge dial metrics.
